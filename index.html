  • MixerDLL is a simple wrapper of Microsofts WASAPI to manage the volumes of different programmes running on your computer. With MixerDLL you can easily develop your own software to control the loudness of individual programs.

Software

  • fritzbox-missed-calls-telegram-bot will inform you about (fixed-line/land line) calls you missed via the telegram-messenger by polling your AVM FritzBox (router that also handles telephony). If available, it also displays the name associated with the phone number so that you know who tried to call you - without even beeing at home. The name comes from a configurable dictionary and by checking several websites that allow reverse lookups of phone numbers.
